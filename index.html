Research and Education

Eames received his Ph.D. in nuclear astrophysics and his M.S. in physics from Texas A&M University in 2020 and 2016, respectively. His graduate and postdoctoral research centered on nuclear astrophysics and nuclear structure. Currently, he puts those skills to use in solving national and global security problems.

Outreach

As a strong proponent for science literacy, Eames frequently engages in outreach opportunities. He was a 2022 Citizen Science faculty member for the Citizen Science program at Bard College and is available for engagement as a science educator and lecturer.

About Me

In his spare time, Eames tries to spend as much time outdoors as possible. He can usually be found in the mountains of New Mexico and Colorado biking, backpacking, skiing, or hiking with his partner and their dogs, Bee and Junebug.
